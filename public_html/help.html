Support

If you phone is not working or there is a problem with the internal system, bring it to one of our locations and a certified employee will look at it and diagnose the problem for free. If your phone is in need of being replaced, simply return your old phone as long as it is in acceptable condition (conditions on separate page) and receive a new Pear Phone on us!

For information on returns/replacements please call customer service at 1-800-5586-44485. For more information on the Pear Phone Ultimate please see the information page. The link to the information page can be found at the top of this page.
